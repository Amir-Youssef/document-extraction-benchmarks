================================================================================
TECHNICAL DETAILS BY MODEL
================================================================================

--- GEMINI FAMILY (Google) ---
- Gemini 3.1 Pro: Latest Pro version configured. Better stability and
  greater reasoning capability. Native PDF support.
- Gemini 3 Flash: Standard model optimized for cost and speed.

--- QWEN FAMILY (Alibaba Cloud) ---
- Qwen 3.5 27B: Dense 27B parameter model from Qwen v3.5 family.
  Implemented via dedicated 4vants vLLM endpoint. High visual performance.
- Qwen 3.5 35B (A3B): MoE architecture with 35B total parameters, but only
  ~3B active per token. Very low latency despite nominal size.
  Implemented via dedicated 4vants vLLM endpoint.
- Qwen 3.5 9B: Compact dense 9B parameter model. Fastest in the
  Qwen 3.5 family. Implemented via dedicated 4vants vLLM endpoint.
- Qwen3-VL: Alibaba's multimodal variant, primarily developed for
  image understanding applied to documents and complex layouts.
- Qwen3-VL 30B: 30B parameter multimodal variant. Greater visual capacity
  than base Qwen3-VL. Served via Ollama.

--- KIMI FAMILY (Moonshot AI) ---
- Kimi K2.5: Massive MoE architecture with 1 trillion parameters. Specialist
  in handling large data volumes in context. Sends PDF as PNG.

--- GEMMA FAMILY (Google Open Models) ---
- Gemma 3 27B: Mid-size open model. Uses SigLIP encoder (v3)
  specialized in high-resolution visual interpretation.
- Gemma 3 12B: Compact and fast version, optimized for low latency.

================================================================================
SERVING INFRASTRUCTURE
================================================================================

1. GOOGLE AI API     -> Gemini (3.1 Pro / Flash)
2. VERTEX AI (MG)    -> Kimi K2.5, Qwen3-VL, Gemma 3 27B/12B

4. OLLAMA (4vants)   -> Qwen3-VL, Qwen3-VL 30B, Gemma 3 27B/12B
